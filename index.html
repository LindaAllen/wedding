Engagement


On September 3, 2016 Ryan and Linda went to NYC for the long weekend, staying at the W downtown. After an exciting day of Broadway and walking the High Line, Ryan and Linda went back to Midtown for dinner. There in Grand Central under the lighted Clock, Ryan proposed to Linda. Linda answered with an “Are you serious?” following with an immediate “Yes!” They then celebrated at Cipriani Dolci, which overlooks Grand Central.
